Who We Are

MoCo Warriors is a non-profit 501 (c)(3) founded in 2009 by Kerwin Stephens to advance the educational and athletic goals of local students while mentoring them to achieve their goals and overcome life’s obstacles. Since the program’s founding, many former MoCo Warriors have gone on to graduate college, with one player even being drafted to the NBA.

Coaching Philosophy

Speed Endurance Elite Scoring Defensive Intensity Sharp Ball Skills

The MoCo Warriors coaching staff uses our own brand of building basketball skill based on a SEEDS principle. Through SEEDS, we’ve helped players compete for positions on their high school team and receive offers to compete at the collegiate level.
